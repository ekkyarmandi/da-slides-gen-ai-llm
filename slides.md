### Key Takeaways

- **Generative AI** creates new content (text, images, code) from learned patterns <!-- element style="line-height: 1.8; margin-bottom: 0.5rem;" -->
- **LLMs** are specialized Gen AI models focused on understanding and generating human-like text <!-- element style="line-height: 1.8; margin-bottom: 0.5rem;" -->
- **Practical Applications** span across industries - from creative automation to customer service <!-- element style="line-height: 1.8; margin-bottom: 0.5rem;" -->
- **Ethical Use** requires both developers and users to prioritize transparency, accuracy, and human agency <!-- element style="line-height: 1.8; margin-bottom: 0.5rem;" -->
